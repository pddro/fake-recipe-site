One-Pot Tomato Rice

Prep: 5 min · Cook: 20 min · Serves 4

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups long-grain white rice
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1.5 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Cook onion until soft, about 3 minutes. Add garlic and cumin, stir for 30 seconds.
  2. Add rice and stir to coat in oil for 1 minute.
  3. Pour in diced tomatoes and broth. Season with salt and pepper. Bring to a boil.
  4. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15 minutes. Do not lift the lid.
  5. Remove from heat and let sit covered for 5 minutes. Fluff with a fork, top with cilantro, and serve.
