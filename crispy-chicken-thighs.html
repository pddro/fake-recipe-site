Crispy Chicken Thighs

Prep: 5 min · Cook: 30 min · Serves 4

Ingredients

  • 4 bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • Salt and black pepper
  • Lemon wedges for serving

Instructions

  1. Pat chicken thighs completely dry with paper towels. Season generously on both sides with garlic powder, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  2. Heat olive oil in a cast-iron skillet over medium-high heat until shimmering.
  3. Place chicken skin-side down. Press gently with a spatula. Cook without moving for 12-15 minutes until skin is deeply golden and crisp.
  4. Flip and cook another 10-12 minutes until internal temperature reaches 165F.
  5. Rest 5 minutes before serving with lemon wedges.
